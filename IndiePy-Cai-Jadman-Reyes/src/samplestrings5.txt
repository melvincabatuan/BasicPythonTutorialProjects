<h>Quiz for Strings Part V</h>

<p>1. A programmer wants to turn a set of strings to lowercase. What method should he use? </p>
<rb>a. str.islower()
<rb>b. str.lower()
<rb>c. str.capitalize()
<rb>d. str.upper()
<c>b

<br>
<p>2.The programmer wants to split a multiline string by line. What method should he use? </p>
<rb>a. str.split()
<rb>b. str.splitlines() 
<rb>c. str.partition()
<rb>d. str.strip()      
<c>b


<br>
<p>3. Which of the following is the correct way of joining a list of comma separated strings stored in variable x?   </p>
<rb>a. ", ".join(x)
<rb>b. x.join()
<rb>c. x.join(", ")
<rb>d. "".join(x)
<c>a
