<h3>Logical Operators</h3>
<p>Logical operators are words used to combine two or more expressions to form a compound expression.</p>

<br>

<br>
<h5>Example</h5>
<img src="LogicalOperators2.png" width="800">

<br>
<h5>Sample Code</h5>
<example>
x = True
y = False

print(x and y)		
print(x or y)		
print(not x)		
print(not (x or y))
</example>

<br>
<h5>Output</h5>
<example>
False
True
False
False
</example>
